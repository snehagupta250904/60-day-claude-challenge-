Role = lens, not just tone

Assigning a persona doesn't just change how Claude sounds — it changes what it prioritises, what it omits, and what vocabulary it uses.

Same question, divergent answers

The Founder focused on market and growth; the Developer on systems and scale. Both answered correctly — just through different frames.

4 components make a complete prompt

Role + Audience + Task + Constraints. Each layer adds precision. Missing layers leave Claude guessing.

Specificity beats generality

"You are a McKinsey consultant" outperforms "You are a business expert" — the more specific the role, the more targeted and useful the output.

Outputs are saveable

The 3-persona demo included a "Save all outputs" button that downloads a .txt comparison file for offline reference.
